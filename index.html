good to know

Your questions, answered.

How long does a website take?

Landing pages usually launch in about a week. Standard sites take roughly 2–3 weeks, and Premium projects vary with scope. You'll always get a clear timeline before we start.

Do you do 24-hour turnaround websites?

Yes! A 24-hour turnaround is possible for the right project. The price depends on the scope and what's realistically achievable in that window, so let's chat and I'll tell you honestly whether it's doable for what you need.

What do you need from me to begin?

Just your goals, any brand bits you already have (logo, colors, content), and a quick chat. Don't have those yet? No problem, I'll guide you through it.

What if I need updates after launch?

Every package offers optional monthly care. I'll handle hosting, content swaps, small updates, and fixes so you never have to touch the code. Prefer to edit things yourself? We can set that up too.

Do you offer payment plans?

Absolutely. Projects usually begin with a deposit, with the balance due at launch. Larger projects can be split into milestones.

What about hosting and my domain?

I'll help you get set up with reliable hosting and connect your domain, and I can keep it all running smoothly with an ongoing care plan.
